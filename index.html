Research Interest

My research focuses on data-driven decision-making under uncertainty (e.g. Bayesian Optimization), especially how we can apply them in many realworld problems such as drug discovery (e.g. for penicillin, Covid, Cancer) or provide theoretical guarantees for their performance. I love the research that is motivated by statistical intuition and be generalized and made scalable by modern machine learning techniques, such as few-shot Bayesian optimization facilitated by knowledge transfer using MDP-prior-fitted neural networks.
